========== STYLE (paste into Suno style box) ==========

Hindi devotional bhajan in Raag Malkauns, karuna-rasa abduction climax with maya. Lead vocal: warm soulful male baritone, slightly nasal resonance, plaintive heart-tugging timbre, restrained vibrato breaking into anguish on Sita-haran, mid-to-low range, earnest sincere delivery, smooth legato, vintage Hindi film bhajan playback style, kathavachak storyteller tone. Slow building tempo through Ravana-Maricha plot, golden-deer ruse, Sita's enchantment, Ram's pursuit, Maricha's death-cry mimicking Ram, Lakshman drawing protective line, Ravana as sannyasi, Sita-haran, Jatayu's heroic stand and fall. Instruments: bansuri eerie on maya, sarangi on Sita-grief, pakhawaj on Ravana's force, tabla, harmonium pad. Production: warm acoustic, deep reverb on abduction peak, vintage devotional aesthetic, no electronics, forest atmosphere with sky-flight.
