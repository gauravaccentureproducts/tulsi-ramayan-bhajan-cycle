Hindi devotional bhajan in Raag Bhairav, deep emotional reunion with resolution. Lead vocal: warm soulful male baritone, slightly nasal resonance, plaintive heart-tugging timbre, restrained vibrato deepening into brotherly love, mid-to-low range, earnest sincere delivery, smooth legato, vintage Hindi film bhajan playback style, kathavachak storyteller tone. Tender build-up tempo through Bharat's approach, emotional climax at Bharat-milap, anguish at father's death news, dharma-debate, gentle resolution at padukas-giving, settling reverent at Bharat returning to Nandigram. Instruments: bansuri prominent on emotional moments, sarangi on Bharat's lament, tabla, harmonium pad, soft conch and bells on padukas-coronation moment, light male chorus on samaapan. Production: warm acoustic, natural reverb on milap moment, vintage devotional aesthetic, no electronics, mountain-ashram atmosphere shifting to Ayodhya at end.
