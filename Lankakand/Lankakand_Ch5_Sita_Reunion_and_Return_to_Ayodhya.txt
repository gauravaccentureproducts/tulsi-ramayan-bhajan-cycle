========== STYLE (paste into Suno style box) ==========

Hindi devotional bhajan transitioning from Raag Marwa to Yaman, shringar-shanta reunion and homecoming. Lead vocal: warm soulful male baritone, slightly nasal resonance, plaintive heart-tugging timbre, restrained vibrato deepening into devotion and joy, mid-to-low range, earnest tender delivery, smooth legato, vintage Hindi film bhajan playback style, kathavachak storyteller tone. Slow building to homecoming celebration tempo. Verses move through Hanuman sent to fetch Sita, Sita's joy at Ram-victory, palanquin to court, Ram's strange request for agni-pariksha, Sita's calm acceptance, Agni-deva returning her unharmed, joyful reunion, Pushpak-viman to Ayodhya, Bharat-milap at Nandigram, Ayodhya welcomes lord home, coronation preparation. Instruments: bansuri prominent on tender moments, sarangi on agni-pariksha, conch on Pushpak arrival, full ensemble at Bharat-milap, tabla, harmonium pad. Production: warm acoustic, natural reverb, vintage devotional-shringar aesthetic, no electronics.
