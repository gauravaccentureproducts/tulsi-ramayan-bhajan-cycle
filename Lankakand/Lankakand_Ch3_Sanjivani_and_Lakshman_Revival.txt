========== STYLE (paste into Suno style box) ==========

Hindi devotional bhajan in Raag Marwa-Bhairavi, karuna grief and miracle revival. Lead vocal: warm soulful male baritone, slightly nasal resonance, plaintive heart-tugging timbre, restrained vibrato breaking into anguish at Ram's grief, mid-to-low range, earnest sincere delivery, smooth legato, vintage Hindi film bhajan playback style, kathavachak storyteller tone. Slow on grief, brisk on Hanuman's flight. Verses move through Ram lamenting fallen Lakshman, Sushen prescribing Sanjivani, Hanuman volunteering, his flight to Dronagiri, lifting the whole mountain when unable to identify, Bharat intercepting and apologizing, returning with mountain, Lakshman revived, vanara celebration, battle resumes with Meghnad's secret yagna interrupted. Instruments: sarangi prominent on lament, pakhawaj on Hanuman's flight, conch on revival, tabla, harmonium pad, bansuri. Production: warm acoustic, reverb on revival moment, vintage devotional aesthetic, no electronics.
