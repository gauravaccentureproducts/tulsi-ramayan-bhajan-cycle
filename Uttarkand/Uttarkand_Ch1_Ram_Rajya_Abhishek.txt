========== STYLE (paste into Suno style box) ==========

Hindi devotional bhajan in Raag Yaman, shanti-rasa coronation grandeur and full-circle joy. Lead vocal: warm soulful male baritone, slightly nasal resonance, plaintive heart-tugging timbre, restrained vibrato deepening into devotion and majesty, mid-to-low range, earnest sincere delivery, smooth legato, vintage Hindi film bhajan playback style, kathavachak storyteller tone. Medium vilambit tempo on chorus opening to celebration. Verses move through Vashishtha preparing the coronation, water from four directions, the abhishek with vedic mantras, deva flower-shower, citizen celebration, brothers honored, Vibhishan and Sugriva-Hanuman taking leave, Hanuman wanting to stay, Ram embracing him, Ram-rajya begins with prosperity. Instruments: shehnai prominent throughout the coronation, conch on abhishek, sarangi for emotion, tabla, harmonium pad. Production: warm acoustic, epic reverb on coronation, vintage devotional-grandeur aesthetic, no electronics.
