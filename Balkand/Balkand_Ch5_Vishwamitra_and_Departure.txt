========== STYLE (paste into Suno style box) ==========

Hindi devotional bhajan in Raag Yaman, reverent shift to vir-rasa preparation. Lead vocal: warm soulful male baritone, slightly nasal resonance, plaintive heart-tugging timbre, restrained vibrato deepening into Dasharath's anguish, then resolve, mid-to-low range, earnest sincere delivery, smooth legato, vintage Hindi film bhajan playback style, kathavachak storyteller tone. Medium tempo on chorus, slowing for Dasharath-vyakulta, building suspense at vana-yatra and approach to Tadaka-forest. Verses move through Vishwamitra's arrival at Dasharath's court, his request for Ram-Lakshman, Dasharath's reluctance, Vashishtha's counsel, princes' departure, Bala-Atibala mantras, crossing Sarayu, reaching Tadaka forest. Instruments: tabla, harmonium pad, sarangi on Dasharath-anguish, bansuri on departure, light pakhawaj building at vana-yatra. Production: warm acoustic, natural reverb, vintage devotional aesthetic, no electronics, court-to-forest atmosphere.
