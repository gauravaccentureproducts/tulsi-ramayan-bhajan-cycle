========== STYLE (paste into Suno style box) ==========

Hindi devotional bhajan in Raag Yaman building dramatic peak then opening into utsav celebration. Lead vocal: warm soulful male baritone, slightly nasal resonance, plaintive heart-tugging timbre, restrained vibrato expanding into full-throated projection at climax, mid-to-low range opening to broad cinematic delivery, earnest dramatic phrasing, smooth legato, vintage Hindi film bhajan playback style, kathavachak storyteller tone. Building tempo through dhanush-yajna, thunderous climax at dhanush-bhang, taut Parashuram-confrontation, jubilant baraat-tempo for chaturvivah. Verses move through dhanush-yajna, kings fail, Janak's lament, Pinaka breaks, varmala, Parashuram arrival, Ram pacifies, weddings, baraat return. Instruments: shehnai blazing for climax and weddings, nagara dhol for percussion, full tabla, sitar, sarangi, harmonium pad, choral voices on jay-ghosh. Production: warm acoustic with epic reverb, vintage devotional aesthetic, no electronics.
