Hindi devotional bhajan in Raag Yaman, karuna-rasa softening into shringar second half. Lead vocal: warm soulful male baritone, slightly nasal resonance, plaintive heart-tugging timbre, restrained vibrato deepening into longing on Sita-darshan verses, mid-to-low range, earnest tender delivery, smooth legato, vintage Hindi film bhajan playback style, kathavachak intimate warmth. Medium tender tempo on chorus and Ahalya-uddhar arc, lingering compassion through Ahalya-stuti and pati-milan, wonder at Mithila-pravesh, softly opening into shringar at Sita-aagaman and pratham-darshan, hushed reverence at Gauri-prarthana. Instruments: bansuri prominent on emotional moments and first-glance, sitar arpeggios on nayan-mile, sarangi on Ahalya-stuti, harmonium pad, light tabla, soft female chorus aalap when Sita appears. Production: warm acoustic, breathtaking stillness on first-darshan, vintage devotional-romantic aesthetic, no electronics, dawn-temple atmosphere.
