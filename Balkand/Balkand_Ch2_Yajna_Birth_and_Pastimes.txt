========== STYLE (paste into Suno style box) ==========

Hindi devotional bhajan in Raag Yaman, joyful mangal-utsav celebration mood. Lead vocal: warm soulful male baritone, slightly nasal resonance, plaintive heart-tugging timbre, restrained vibrato, mid-to-low range, earnest sincere delivery, smooth legato, vintage Hindi film bhajan playback style, kathavachak storyteller warmth. Medium kaharwa tempo on chorus and yajna-aagaman, celebratory build through yajna-purusha and kheer-vitaran, jubilant burst at Ram-janma and char-bhrata, soften to tender lullaby register for bal-leela and bandhu-kreeda, calm rising at gurukul-shiksha and samaapan. Instruments: shehnai prominent (birth and naamkaran), tabla, dholak for festive pulse, harmonium pad, temple bells and conch on mangal moments, light male chorus echoes on jay-jaykar, soft bansuri on bal-leela. Production: warm acoustic, festive reverb, vintage devotional aesthetic, no electronics, courtyard celebration atmosphere.
