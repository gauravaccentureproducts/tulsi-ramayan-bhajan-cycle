Hindi devotional bhajan in Raag Yaman with vir-rasa heroic energy, demon-slaying climax. Lead vocal: warm soulful male baritone, slightly nasal resonance, plaintive heart-tugging timbre, restrained vibrato shifting to declamatory force in action verses, mid-to-low range opening to broader projection, earnest dramatic delivery, smooth legato, vintage Hindi film bhajan playback style, kathavachak storyteller tone. Medium-brisk dadra-kaharwa tempo, dramatic surge at Tadaka encounter, explosive vir-climax at Tadaka-vadh, settling triumphant after Subahu-vadh and yajna-completion. Verses move through Tadaka emergence, Tadaka-vadh, divyastra-pradan, Siddhashram, Maricha thrown and Subahu burnt, yajna complete. Instruments: pakhawaj and dholak driving battle intensity, sarangi for tension, tabla, restrained shehnai at yajna scenes, sitar accents on astra-pradan, harmonium pad. Production: warm acoustic with cinematic depth, vintage devotional-heroic aesthetic, no electronics.
