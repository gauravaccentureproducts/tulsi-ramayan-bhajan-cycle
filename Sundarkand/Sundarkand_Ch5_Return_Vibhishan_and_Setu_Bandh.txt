========== STYLE (paste into Suno style box) ==========

Hindi devotional bhajan in Raag Hamir-Bhairavi, victorious return and Vibhishan-sharanagati. Lead vocal: warm soulful male baritone, slightly nasal resonance, plaintive heart-tugging timbre, restrained vibrato deepening with joy and resolve, mid-to-low range, earnest sincere delivery, smooth legato, vintage Hindi film bhajan playback style, kathavachak storyteller tone. Building tempo from celebration to resolve. Verses move through Hanuman flying back, vanara joy, reaching Ram with Sita-message and chudamani, Ram's grief and resolve, marching to ocean, Ravana exiling Vibhishan, Vibhishan surrendering to Ram, Ram crowning him future-king, Setu-bandh begins with Nala-Nila and Ram-named stones. Instruments: full ensemble on vanara joy, sarangi on Ram-grief, conch on setu-bandh, tabla, harmonium pad, bansuri. Production: warm acoustic, epic reverb on coronation, vintage devotional aesthetic, no electronics.
